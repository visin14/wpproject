Rajasthan

The Land of Kings

Rajasthan, the largest state in India, is known for its majestic forts, palaces, and desert landscapes.

Hawa Mahal

The "Palace of Winds" in Jaipur, known for its unique architecture.

Amber Fort

A stunning fort overlooking Maota Lake, showcasing Rajput architecture.

Lake Palace

A luxurious palace located on Lake Pichola in Udaipur.

Jaisalmer Fort

One of the largest living forts in the world, located in the golden sands of the Thar Desert.
