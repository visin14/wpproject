Delhi

The Heart of India

Delhi, India's capital territory, is a massive metropolitan area blending ancient history and modern development.

Red Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of India's rich historical past.

Qutub Minar

The tallest brick minaret in the world and an excellent example of Indo-Islamic architecture.

India Gate

A war memorial dedicated to the soldiers of the British Indian Army who died in World War I.

Humayun's Tomb

The tomb of the Mughal Emperor Humayun, commissioned by his wife in 1569-70.
