Kullu Manali

The Valley of Gods

Kullu Manali, a paradise for nature lovers, is known for its snow-capped mountains, adventure sports, and breathtaking landscapes.

Rohtang Pass

A high-altitude mountain pass offering stunning views and adventure activities.

Solang Valley

A popular destination for skiing, paragliding, and other adventure sports.

Manikaran Sahib

A sacred pilgrimage site famous for its hot springs and religious significance.

Hadimba Temple

An ancient wooden temple dedicated to Goddess Hadimba, surrounded by deodar forests.
